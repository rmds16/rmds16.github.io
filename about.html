About Us
Codicote Motors Ltd, formerly David Thomas Garages.

After 12 years as service manager, Jon Lawrence was offered the opportunity to purchase the well established David Thomas Garages and to continue great customer service.

We at Codicote Motors are here to take care of all your motors needs from a bulb to an engine rebuild. With fully Bosch trained technicians and the latest diagnostic equipment.

We offer great value servicing and repairs to all cars and light commercials carried out to the highest standards using quality parts. We are RMI, Trust My Garage and AA certified. We offer a local collection and delivery service as well as courtesy cars upon request. Give us a call for a quote.
